I lead startup partnerships at Microsoft, working with founders to take AI and infrastructure ideas from prototype to production on Azure.

What I do

My day-to-day is spent embedded with early-stage and growth-stage companies in the Microsoft for Startups program. I help technical founders pick the right Azure architecture, unblock their teams on platform decisions, and connect them to the people inside Microsoft who can move things forward, whether that is capacity, credits, model access, or product feedback that actually lands with the right team.

The work spans frontier AI labs, vertical SaaS, devtools, and infrastructure. The common thread is shortening the distance between a founder having an idea and that idea running reliably in front of customers.

Where I focus

  • Access

  • Feedback

  • Enablement
